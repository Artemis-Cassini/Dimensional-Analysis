About the Calculator

    When I was introduced to this chemistry concept at the beginning of the year, I was okay with it, it was easy I liked it a lot then. Then we got into semester 2. That is where I learned how much I HATE the concept. I wanted to cry, I hated it that much. I never wanted to see it again, but every chapter had the concept. I thought it would never go away. But then I had an idea, an idea that changed everything.

    I love to build random project, for me, for others, or anyone really. So I decided to create, the dimensional analysis calculator. This has been my passion project for months, and it has really sparked now. I spent hours, days, weeks, and months building this calculator and adding as many conversions as I could. I learned a lot during this, how to use a regex for my molar masses, how to set up Render, how to enter a virtural space, and how to use Git. This calculator is my best project by far. The most interesting one, the most difficult one, and the most time consuming one, and it's the favorite. I'm proud to be the creator of this calculator, and I hope you all enjoy this wonderful, never ending project.
