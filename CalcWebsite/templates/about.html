About the Calculator

    Believe it or not, I actually HATE dimensional analysis, a lot. But I really like to build things, so I made this calculator to help me, and others who also may hate it. Dimensional Analysis is a concept I learned this year in chemistry, and I recall my teacher saying at the beginning of the year "we will never use this again" and then we did. Everything in the second half of the year was dimensional analysis, and I wanted to cry.

    So, I created this calculator out of hate for the concept, and passion for the project at hand. Not only has this project allow me to build a portfolio, but it's also helped me earn opportunities at places such as NASA. Honestly, I still do hate dimensional analysis, but this calculator has made me feel better about it. I'm thankful for everyone who has supported me and this project.

    Special thanks to my sister for helping me with some of the coding.

    Special thanks to Nina Faller for being a wonderful chemistry teacher!

    Special thanks to my dog, Poppy, without her there next to me I would've gone insane.

    Special thanks to all my classmates who have supported me throughout this project.
