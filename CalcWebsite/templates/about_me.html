About Me

    My name is Artemis (not really just my online name) and I hate dimensional analysis. So I built this instead.

    I've been coding for a while now, and this is my first really big, public project. I have worked really hard on this calculator for myself, and for others. I am proud to present it to you today. I guess I should talk a little bit more about myself and who I am.

    I'm into a lot of things, like tech, music, space, building things, and learning. I've actually been learning more advanced chemistry on my own for awhile now. I've learned a lot from it along with the many other things I study in my free time. I'm really glad I was able to create this for everyone who shares the same opinion as me. I hope everyone enjoys it as much as I do!
