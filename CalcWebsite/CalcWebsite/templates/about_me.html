Dimensional Analysis Solutions

Builder • Student • Curious about everything

Hi there, I'm Artemis, and I hate dimensional analysis. Yet, I hate it that much that I built my very own calculator on it. I spent so much time working on this calculator I might as well yap about myself as well.

I do not just want DAS to solve problems. I want it to help people understand them.

This project really matters to me because it is not just a calculator. I want it to be something that helps people learn while also understanding dimensional analysis. I love building things that are useful, and I love when I can turn a project into something that helps people understand and not confuse them. I want to continue to improve DAS and keep it as a life long project that continues to get worked on for many years.

I am a young creator of many things. I mainly code a bunch of random projects that never get published or I code for school, however, this is a project that really meant a lot to me. I've been coding for maybe 3-4 years now and I typically work in Python and HTML. I'm still learning a lot about CSS and Rust but I do a little bit of work in both. I try to really get in touch with the subject that I'm studying and I like to go in depth about what I learn. I plan to major in electrical engineering and/or computer engineering. I also plan to keep a minor in music. I am currently taking online college classes however I will be attending real life college very soon. I plan to update this calculator (I haven't in a very long time) but that is going to change as this is a project I want to use. I love hearing all the feedback that anyone has to offer, whether if it's changes or just genuine praise. I'm glad to be able to make this and share it with the people. Thanks for reading c:

I am always learning, always looking, and always looking to make my projects the best they can be. DAS is only a small part of that, but I care about this project like a child. I'm excited to see DAS keep growing over time.
